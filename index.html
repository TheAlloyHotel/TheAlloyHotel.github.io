    Fitness Center

    located on the Lobby Level past the bar to the left, is available 24/7 with guest room key access.
    Features two Peloton stationary bikes and Precore equipment.

    Housekeeping Services:

    • Extra towels and toiletries are available at the Front Desk or can be delivered to your guest room.
    • Requests for housekeeping service can be made by contacting our CARE Line on your guest room phone or by visiting the Front Desk.

    Shuttle Service:

    Available to any location within 5 miles, including your local office if applicable.
    Contact the CARE Line, Front Desk, or email your Alloy point of contact directly to setup a time in advance.

    Web Access:

    Available to all guests of the hotel through Hilton's guest verification platform.
    When connecting on your mobile device, simply enter the room number and the last name of the primary guest of record.
    NOT CURRENTLY AVAILABLE FOR WIRED GAMING CONSOLES

    Laundry Services:

    Available to all guests of the hotel for a standard fee.
    Simply reach out to any front office staff member at The Alloy.

    Pet fees:

    The Alloy Hotel is pet friendly!
    There is a non-refundable pet deposit of $50 per pet due at check-in

    Questions

    We want to make sure all of your needs are met.
    Feel free to reach out to Parnell.Kelley@hilton.com for all things related to guest experience.
